How it works

01

Register Property

First, you Start your registration process online, by clicking the 'Register' button.

02

Enter Details

You enter your Details and the Property's Details as asked in the Registrations page.

03

Finalize Registration

We will then Verify the details you provided from our end, Just to check everything are in order.

04

Get your E-Key

Finally, your details will get added to the Registry, and the Hash of your very own block will be given to you as your E-Key
